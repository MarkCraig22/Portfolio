Bike Sales

In an Excel project focusing on bike sales, I employed pivot tables, line graphs, and bar graphs to analyze sales data comprehensively. Through interactive filtering by region, marital status, and education, the project aimed to uncover nuanced bike sale trends and target specific audiences effectively. By dissecting the data using Excel's functionalities, actionable insights emerged, shedding light on regional preferences, demographic buying behaviors, and the impact of education and marital status on purchase decisions. This project underscores my proficiency in leveraging Excel for data analysis and visualization, empowering businesses to strategize effectively, tailor marketing efforts, and maximize sales potential.

Tableau Dashboards

In addition to exploring and preparing the data from the Brazil travel dataset using SQL queries, I utilized the insights gained to create three comprehensive dashboards highlighting key metrics and trends for users, flights, and hotels. These dashboards showcase statistics and trends over time, offering valuable insights into user behavior, flight patterns, and hotel bookings. By visualizing the data in these dashboards, I provided a comprehensive overview of the travel landscape in Brazil, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions based on the presented metrics and trends. This project demonstrates my proficiency and showcases my ability to translate insights into actionable visualizations for effective decision-making.
